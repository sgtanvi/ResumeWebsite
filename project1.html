Rush Hour Study App

Code Base

Description

The goal of Rush Hour Study app was to motivate students to do interval studying instead of cramming. My team and I were inspired by the Pomodoro technique (interval studying). We implemented an reward system as well to motivate students using with cute icons based on food


Project Details and Contributions

  • Implemented the application for readability, scalability, and efficiency (improved by 25%)
  • Spearheaded collaboration between fellow teammates to develop an Android application that employed the pomodoro technique to help students focus.
  • Technologies used: Java, Android App Studios, Emulator, and CSS, MySQL
