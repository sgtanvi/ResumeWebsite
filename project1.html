Rush Hour Study App

Code Base

Description

The goal of the app was to motivate students to do interval studying instead of cramming. My team and I were inspired by the Pomodoro technique (interval studying). We implemented an reward system as well to motivate students using with cute icons based on food


Features

  • Create a shopping list (createShoppingList, showList & addToList)
  • Assign store to user (setStore)
  • Add items to list (addItems & showItems)
  • Simple calculator functions
