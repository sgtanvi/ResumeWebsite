The Space Between: Meditation Art Piece

An web art piece to connect each other virtually through meditation and art, coded to bring our buddies together

Code Base

Description

Connect with others through the internet in a meditating way, to feel less alone in this digital space. Inspired by the app Headspace, wellness, and connection from within and with others. 3d objects inspired by the Four Bodies by Jill Willard of our four distinct parts- physical, emotional, spiritual to bring about a physical presence in our digital real and bring awareness to our bodies as we meditate in this space between the screen and another across it.


Project Details and Contributions

  • Second place in Design and Visual Concept in hackathon for Software & Computer Engineering Society (improved by 25%)
  • Designed a single webpage prototype that plays music while one meditates with others in the room
  • Utilized Adobe-Illustrator, Adobe-XD, and SolidWorks to 3D model and design
  • Technologies used: Javascript, React.js, three.js, json
